The ancient capital of Hue

An ancient city, the ancient capital of the Nguyen Dynasty - here you can explore the mausoleum, Hue citadel, pagodas and the romantic Perfume River.

Ba Na Hills

Ba Na Hills is known as "Da Lat of Central", "Road to Fairyland", with many beautiful landscapes, 1 day climate with 4 full seasons: spring, summer, autumn, winter.

Linh Ung Pagoda

Da Nang has 3 pagodas named Linh Ung. Linh Ung Pagoda Bai But Son is the largest and most beautiful temple and has great spiritual value for the people of Da Nang.
